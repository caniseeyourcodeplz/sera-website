OUR STORY

Built on the calls that never got answered.

Sera started with a simple observation: the most critical moment for a healthcare practice — a patient calling to book — was being lost to voicemail, hold times, and staff bandwidth. We built the system that should have existed.

FOUNDING STORY

Every clinic had the same story.

Every clinic we talked to had the same story. A patient calls at 6:30pm. It goes to voicemail. They call a competitor at 6:31pm. That's not a staffing problem — it's an infrastructure problem.

We built Sera to be the perfect first voice a patient hears. Not a phone tree. Not a chatbot. A voice that sounds human, asks the right questions, and hands off a clean summary to staff — every time, at any hour.

We're not building a feature. We're building the operating system for the modern healthcare front desk.
